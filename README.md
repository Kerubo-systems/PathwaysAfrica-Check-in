# 🌱 Pathways Africa Check-in

> *Supporting students. Empowering mentors. One check-in at a time.*

## Welcome!

Applying to university is exciting, but it can also be overwhelming.

Between essays, financial aid, deadlines, self-doubt, and trying to tell your story, it's easy to feel like you're navigating the journey alone. **Pathways Check-in** was created to make that journey a little less lonely.

This platform provides a simple, safe space for students to regularly check in with how they're doing, while giving mentors meaningful insights into the needs of their cohorts. Rather than waiting until someone asks for help, mentors can better understand common challenges, celebrate progress, and offer timely support where it's needed most.

At its heart, this project is about listening.

---

## 💙 Why this project exists

One conversation can reveal a lot.

While speaking with fellow students, it became clear that many shared similar feelings:

* Feeling overwhelmed by college applications.
* Struggling to find their story.
* Unsure where to begin with essays.
* Balancing applications with school and family responsibilities.
* Wondering if everyone else had it all figured out.

Those conversations inspired this project.

Instead of relying on occasional feedback, **Pathways Check-in** creates an ongoing way for students to share how they're doing and for mentors to better understand the overall wellbeing of their cohorts.

---

## 🌍 What Pathways Check-in does

Students can:

* Complete a quick and friendly check-in.
* Share how they're feeling.
* Rate their confidence in the application process.
* Highlight the areas they're finding most challenging.
* Request the kinds of support they would find most helpful.
* Share a little about themselves beyond applications.
* Celebrate small wins along the way.
* Provide anonymous feedback if they choose.

Mentors and coordinators can:

* View real-time cohort insights.
* Identify common challenges.
* Monitor confidence trends.
* Understand what support students need most.
* Compare trends across cohorts and modules.
* Make more informed decisions about workshops, mentorship, and student support.

---

## 🌱 Designed with people in mind

This platform isn't intended to evaluate students.

It isn't another assignment.

It isn't another essay.

It's simply a check-in.

Students are encouraged to answer honestly, skip optional questions if they wish, and remember that there are no "perfect" responses.

Sometimes the most valuable insight is simply knowing that someone needs encouragement.

## ✨ Features

* Multi-step, mobile-friendly check-in experience.
* Warm and accessible user interface.
* Support for multiple modules and cohorts.
* Anonymous or identified submissions.
* Real-time analytics dashboard.
* Role-based administrator access.
* Cohort-specific and program-wide insights.
* Secure backend and scalable architecture.

---

## 🚀 Technology Stack

* **Frontend:** Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui
* **Backend:** Node.js, Express, TypeScript
* **Database:** PostgreSQL with Prisma ORM
* **Authentication:** JWT and bcrypt
* **Validation:** Zod
